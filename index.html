Weather Tracker
Sep 2021
Frustrated with the lack of local opportunities due to the pandemic, I went to my Society of Software Developer's
club and proposed the idea of making a weather tracking app using a weather API. With only one taker, we went to
work immediately and made amazing progress in the few days we gave ourselves! Using JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and
ReactJS, our Weather Tracker takes city name input and dispalays the temperature (including min and max) as well as
a neat icon to match the forcasted weather.
